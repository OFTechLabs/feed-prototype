Feed

The feed allows users to receive information they need instantly, without the need to request for it. It can also monitor progress, tasks, goals, errors, warnings, updates and let's you know when something changes to give a calm state of mind: there is no need to worry about forgetting something important, the feed will always be there!

I'm live at: https://s3.amazonaws.com/feedprototype/

Feel free to try me out right now, I even work on your phone! I also receive regular updates, so be sure to try again once in a while :)

Instantly receive updates

Whenever another user finishes a task, a calculation finishes, new results are ready or new data is available you'll instantly receive an update right here in the feed!

Progressive Web App

The Feed is installable and lives on the user's home screen, without the need for an app store. It offers an immersive full screen experience and can even re-engage users with web push notifications.

We will only show information that's relevant to you! The feed is completely personalized for you and your role, if we ever show something that's not relevant you can let us know and we'll stop showing similar information.

Monitor progress

We'll monitor the progress of tasks and goals and automatically notify you if we're no longer on track or if certain goals are in danger. We'll even give you steps you might take to get back on track! All your colleagues will also receive updates automatically if they are falling behind, along with steps they might take to resolve any issues.
